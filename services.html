Services

We at Which believe that clients wishes are the top priority for our team of interior design consultants. Clients participation is encouraged at all stages during the planning and implementation of the project. Space planning and consequently the entire design project is considered successful only when the team of Which is able to transform clients vision into a built reality.

For any residential or commercial interior design project, our team of professionals is fully responsible for preparation of complete turnkey package which includes all project details from initial conception phase to the final execution. Which provide exceptional services at every level of the project along with overall project management and incorporating interior design changes occurring during work progress.

Which goal is to create a unique and innovative solution that will be a perfect balance between functionality and aesthetics. We emphasize on budget management and systematic scheduling of all activities to ensure smooth and successful completion of all interior design projects exceeding clients expectations. Our team will oversee ordering and tracking of all products, coordination with contractors, delivery, and execution of floor and wall coverings, furniture, light fixtures, etc. and ensure proper maintenance of all sourced objects that are needed for space. We at Which always remain committed to providing the best service to our clients even after the completion of the design project.

Step By Step

  • Identifying the client’s taste & requirements by showcasing our E-presentation

  • Gathering information from the client such as their family size, age, profession, sun sign of persons occupying the place, etc. for better understanding of client’s lifestyle

  • Get measurements from the site, capture the client’s vision and transform it into an interior design plan with theme & concept development

  • Establish project time frames and define budget guidelines

  • Selection of color and material for floor and wall coverings, furniture, soft furnishings, accessories, lighting, white goods, communications and audio-visual design

  • Several options are considered in the selection process of all the materials, colors, top finishes, light fixtures, artwork, etc.

  • Requisites for final budget is established by evaluation of BOQ (quotations) and selection of vendors and contractors

  • Procurement of materials, execution, supervision, and coordination of entire project as per pre-defined schedules

  • Purchasing, constructing, supervision and coordination of entire project

  • Definite and successful project executions

  • Handover the site peacefully to the client
